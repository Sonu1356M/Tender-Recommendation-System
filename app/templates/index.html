About Tender On Time

Tender On Time is an AI-powered tender search platform that helps procurement professionals and organizations find relevant tender opportunities quickly and efficiently.

Our system leverages advanced technologies including:

  • Elasticsearch for vector-based search
  • OpenAI for natural language processing and embeddings
  • Feedback-based refinement for improved relevance

With Tender On Time, you can search through thousands of tender opportunities using simple text queries, filter by country and date range, and provide feedback to continuously improve your search results.
